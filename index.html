Short division — the bus stop method

Short division breaks a large division into small steps. You work through each digit of the dividend (the number being divided) from left to right, dividing by the divisor each time. Any leftover is the remainder, carried into the next column. The digits written above the line form the quotient — the answer.

Worked example: 1 ÷ 4 = 0.25
